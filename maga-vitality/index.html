How do I know if toxins are really my problem?
Symptoms of toxic burden include: brain fog, difficulty losing weight, metallic taste, chemical sensitivity, chronic fatigue despite rest, poor recovery from exercise, and frequent infections. However, if you've lived in a city, eaten conventional food, had dental amalgams, or worked around chemicals, you have toxin accumulation. The question isn't if, but how much.
Will this protocol work if I'm on medications?
Most men on blood pressure, cholesterol, or diabetes medications can safely follow this protocol, but consult your physician. Detoxification may affect medication metabolism, potentially requiring dose adjustments. Many men find they need less medication as metabolism improves. Never stop medications without medical supervision. The protocol complements medical treatment, not replaces it. Inform your doctor about all supplements to check for interactions.
What if I feel worse before feeling better?
The "healing crisis" or Herxheimer reaction is common during detox. As toxins mobilize, symptoms temporarily worsen - fatigue, headaches, skin breakouts, mood swings. This typically lasts 3-7 days and indicates the protocol is working. Support yourself with extra water, rest, vitamin C, and activated charcoal to bind circulating toxins. If symptoms are severe or persist beyond a week, reduce detox supplement doses and extend the protocol timeline.
