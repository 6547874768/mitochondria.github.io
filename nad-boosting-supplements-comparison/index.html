🥉 NR (Nicotinamide Riboside)
★★★★☆ Most established human research

✅ Research Advantages:

  • Most extensive human clinical trial data
  • FDA GRAS (Generally Recognized as Safe) status
  • Available from established supplement companies
  • Consistent manufacturing and quality standards

❌ Research Considerations:

  • Studies show additional conversion steps may reduce efficiency
  • Moderate cost ($80-150/month for research doses)
  • Research indicates modest NAD increases in some studies
  • May require higher doses for noticeable effects
Research Summary:
Well-established safety profile with consistent human research. May be a reliable option but potentially not the most efficient for NAD support.

Research on NAD Pathways

Understanding how different NAD precursors work in the body helps explain why research shows varying effectiveness:

🧬 Research on NAD Biosynthesis Pathways:

Salvage Pathway (Niacinamide): Studies suggest this may be the most efficient route for NAD support. Enhanced formulations may help bypass rate-limiting steps.

Preiss-Handler Pathway (NMN): Research indicates this pathway may face transport and cellular uptake limitations that affect effectiveness.

De Novo Pathway (NR): Studies show additional conversion steps that may reduce overall efficiency compared to direct approaches.
