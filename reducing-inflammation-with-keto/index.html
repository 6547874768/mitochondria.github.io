Blood Sugar Stability and Inflammation Control

Chronic elevated blood glucose creates a pro-inflammatory environment through glycation reactions and oxidative stress. The ketogenic diet addresses this root cause by maintaining stable, low glucose levels while providing clean-burning ketone fuel.

Understanding how keto stabilizes blood sugar reveals why glucose control is fundamental to inflammation management. When blood sugar remains stable, inflammatory triggers decrease dramatically across all body systems.
