5-Step Hormonal Optimization Protocol

  1. Step 1 - Baseline Assessment (Week 1): Test key hormones including insulin, cortisol, thyroid panel (TSH, T3, T4), and sex hormones (testosterone, estrogen, progesterone). This establishes your hormonal starting point and identifies specific imbalances.
  2. Step 2 - Insulin Stabilization (Weeks 2-4): Begin with moderate carb reduction (50-75g daily) to avoid shocking your system. Focus on healthy fats (avocados, olive oil, nuts) while maintaining adequate protein to support hormone synthesis.
  3. Step 3 - Full Ketosis Transition (Weeks 5-8): Reduce carbs to 20-25g daily and implement The Ultimate Keto Meal Plan designed specifically for hormonal health optimization and metabolic flexibility.
  4. Step 4 - Stress Management Integration (Weeks 9-12): Add stress-reduction techniques like meditation, adequate sleep (7-9 hours), and gentle exercise to support healthy cortisol patterns and optimize the HPA axis.
  5. Step 5 - Long-term Hormonal Maintenance (Week 13+): Implement cyclical approaches if needed, monitor hormonal markers every 3 months, and adjust macros based on individual response and life stage changes.
