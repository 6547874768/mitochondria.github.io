Frequently Asked Questions

How does keto affect hormone balance?
Keto improves hormone balance by enhancing insulin sensitivity, reducing inflammation, optimizing cortisol levels, and supporting healthy fat intake needed for hormone production. Studies show significant improvements in reproductive hormones, metabolic markers, and overall endocrine function within 8-12 weeks.
Is keto safe for women's hormones?
When properly implemented, keto is generally safe and beneficial for most women's hormones. It can improve PCOS symptoms, regulate menstrual cycles, and optimize reproductive health. However, individual monitoring and gradual implementation are recommended, especially for women with existing hormonal conditions.
Can keto help with PCOS?
Yes, research shows keto can significantly improve PCOS symptoms by reducing insulin resistance, lowering LH/FSH ratios, and supporting healthy weight management. Studies demonstrate improvements in reproductive hormone levels and metabolic markers within 45-90 days of consistent ketogenic eating.
How should I modify keto for hormonal health?
Focus on hormone-supporting meal plans that include adequate calories, nutrient-dense foods, and strategic timing. Consider cycle syncing for women and regular hormone monitoring for optimal results.
