Frequently Asked Questions

How does keto diet affect women's hormones?
Ketogenic diet can significantly improve hormonal balance in women, especially those with PCOS. Studies show reduced LH/FSH ratio, decreased free testosterone levels, and improved insulin sensitivity.
Is keto safe during menopause?
For most menopausal women, ketogenic diet can help manage symptoms by stabilizing insulin levels and reducing inflammation. However, individual monitoring is essential, especially in the first few months.
Can keto help with PCOS symptoms?
Research demonstrates that ketogenic diet can significantly improve PCOS symptoms by reducing insulin resistance, lowering androgen levels, and helping normalize menstrual cycles.
What about thyroid function on keto?
While some may experience temporary T3 changes during adaptation, most studies show stable or improved thyroid function long-term, especially when combined with targeted thyroid support protocols.
