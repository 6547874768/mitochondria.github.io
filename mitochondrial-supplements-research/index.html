How to Choose Evidence-Based Supplements

  1. Step 1 - Verify Human Clinical Trials: Look for supplements with at least 3-5 published human studies, not just cell or animal research.   2. Step 2 - Confirm Therapeutic Dosage: Match supplement doses to those used in successful clinical trials. CoQ10: 100-200mg ubiquinol. NAD+ precursors: 250-500mg. PQQ: 10-20mg. Under-dosing is the #1 reason supplements fail. Many commercial products contain 10-25% of clinical doses.

  5. Step 5 - Start Single, Then Stack: Begin with one core supplement (usually CoQ10) for 4 weeks to assess response. Add others gradually, monitoring energy and wellbeing. Optimal stacks combine 3-5 synergistic compounds. Document changes to identify what works for you.
