Essential safety practices for pressure cooking:

  • Never fill beyond 2/3 capacity (1/2 for foods that expand)
  • Ensure sealing ring is properly seated before each use
  • Keep hands and face away from steam during pressure release
  • Don't force lid open - wait for float valve to drop
  • Replace sealing ring every 12-18 months or if damaged
  • Use natural release for foamy foods to prevent clogging

Keto-specific considerations: High-fat recipes may foam more; use natural release. Dairy-based sauces can curdle under pressure; add after cooking. Thickeners like xanthan gum should be added after pressure cooking to prevent clumping.
