Are there options for food allergies and dietary restrictions?
Yes! The cookbook includes dairy-free, nut-free, and egg-free variations for most recipes. A comprehensive substitution guide helps you adapt any recipe to your needs. Special icons mark recipes that are naturally free from common allergens.
