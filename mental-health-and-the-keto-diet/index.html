  2. Step 2 - Adaptation Support (Days 15-28): Reduce carbs to 30-50g and prioritize electrolyte balance with sodium (3-5g), potassium (3-4g), and magnesium (400-600mg) daily. Proper mineral status is crucial for neurotransmitter function and mood stability during adaptation.
