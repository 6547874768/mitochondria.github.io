Frequently Asked Questions

How does keto affect hormone balance?
Keto optimizes hormone balance by improving insulin sensitivity by 75%, stabilizing cortisol production, supporting healthy fat-based hormone synthesis, and resetting sex hormone levels (estrogen, progesterone, testosterone). The diet provides essential building blocks for hormone production while reducing inflammation that disrupts endocrine function.
Is keto good for women's hormones?
Yes, keto can significantly benefit women's hormones by reducing estrogen dominance, improving insulin sensitivity, supporting adrenal function, and providing building blocks for hormone production through healthy fats. Results typically appear within 6-12 weeks, with improved PMS, menstrual regularity, and menopausal symptom relief.
Can keto help with PCOS and hormone imbalances?
Absolutely. Research shows keto dramatically improves PCOS by reducing insulin resistance, lowering androgen levels, and improving LH/FSH ratios. Body-type specific approaches can be particularly effective for addressing individual hormonal challenges.
What about thyroid hormones and keto?
Keto can improve thyroid efficiency by reducing inflammation and supporting T4 to T3 conversion. While some studies show temporary T3 reduction, this often reflects improved metabolic efficiency rather than thyroid dysfunction. Proper electrolyte balance and adequate calories support healthy thyroid function on keto.
