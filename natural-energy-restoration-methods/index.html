  • Tai chi provides gentle exercise with proven energy and mood benefits
  • Stretching and mobility work prevents energy-draining muscle tension

Energizing Exercise Strategies

  • Morning movement jumpstarts metabolism and enhances daytime energy
  • Brief activity breaks every hour prevent energy stagnation
  • Outdoor exercise combines movement benefits with nature therapy
  • Strength training builds energy-producing muscle mass

Exercise Timing and Intensity

  • Moderate intensity prevents energy depletion while building stamina

  • Recovery days prevent burnout and support energy restoration
