Understanding How Keto Helps with Blood Sugar Control

The ketogenic diet helps with blood sugar through multiple complementary mechanisms that reduce glucose fluctuations, improve insulin sensitivity, and stabilize energy levels. By drastically reducing carbohydrate intake, keto minimizes the primary driver of blood sugar elevation.

When you eliminate most carbohydrates from your diet, your body experiences fewer glucose spikes, allowing insulin levels to normalize and cellular sensitivity to improve. This fundamental shift creates stable energy production through fat metabolism rather than the glucose rollercoaster of high-carb eating.
