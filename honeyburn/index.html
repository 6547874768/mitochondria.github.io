Understanding Lipase: Your Body's Fat-Burning Engine

To truly appreciate HoneyBurn's innovation, it's essential to understand lipase and its role in weight management. Lipase is produced primarily by the pancreas and is responsible for breaking down triglycerides - the main form of dietary fat - into smaller molecules that can be absorbed and utilized by your body.
