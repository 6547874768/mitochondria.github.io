5-Step Women's Keto Optimization Protocol

  1. Step 1 - Female Foundation (Days 1-7): Begin with gentle carb reduction to 75-100g daily while prioritizing healthy fats essential for hormone production. Track menstrual cycle patterns and energy levels as your body begins adapting to ketogenic fuel for female-specific benefits.
  2. Step 2 - Hormonal Stabilization (Days 8-14): Reduce carbs to 30-50g and maintain precise electrolyte balance with sodium (3-5g), potassium (3-4g), and magnesium (400-600mg) daily. Proper minerals support adrenal function crucial for women's hormone production.
  3. Step 3 - Reproductive Optimization (Days 15-21): Drop to 20-25g carbs daily and implement The Ultimate Keto Meal Plan designed for women's unique nutritional needs and hormone optimization.
  4. Step 4 - Cycle Integration (Days 22-35): Add strategic carb cycling around menstruation, prioritize sleep quality (7-9 hours), and incorporate stress-reduction practices to support healthy cortisol patterns essential for female hormonal balance.
  5. Step 5 - Women's Wellness Mastery (Day 36+): Fine-tune your approach based on menstrual patterns and hormonal responses, maintain therapeutic ketone levels of 1.5-3.0 mmol/L, and adjust for life stages like fertility, pregnancy planning, or menopause.
